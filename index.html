A LITTLE ABOUT PROCYON

Courses taught at Drexel are the university's asset. At Procyon, we believe that searching for courses should be a true exploration, a discovery, if not a "treasure hunt."
For students, numerous courses await to freshen your ordinary academic schedule.
For professors, do you ever wonder whether students know that your class actually exists?
New and delightful courses are added to Drexel schedule system every term. Yet, no one has been given the right tool to discover them.
That's why we want a better search engine to help us explore this "hidden gem" to the fullest.
That's why we create Procyon.

A SEARCH THAT EXPANDS BOUNDARIES

Procyon changes the way you search for courses. Because your curiosity and passion about the unexplored academic horizon matters.
